## Target Users

The primary targeted users of the German Student Navigator are international students who are newly arriving or planning to study in Germany, especially those pursuing Bachelor’s or Master’s degrees. These students often face challenges understanding German administrative systems, accommodation processes, visa requirements, health insurance, banking, and daily communication due to language barriers and fragmented information sources.
